Create & Innovate with Dye Sub Applications

Soft Signage

Eye-catching color and photo-realistic images for interior and backlit signage, banners, indoor flags and hanging graphics with elegant drape.

Photo Panels

Vivid image clarity is infused on specially coated metal panels to create scratch- and abrasion-resistant photo reproductions, tabletops, fine art and more.

Exhibit Graphics

Pop-up displays, trade show graphics and hanging signage are exceptionally durable, easy to install and lightweight for better handling and lower shipping costs.

Fashion/Garments

Wearable fabrics and be customized for everything from high-demand team performance wear to sample textiles for designers and artists creating personalized, branded fashions.

Interior Décor

Upholstery, pillows, window blinds and hard surface decoration are just the beginning of the opportunities to use dye-sub in the quickly expanding décor market segment.

Specialty Items

You can transfer to a wide variety of objects from coffee mugs and mouse pads to snowboards and skateboards.
